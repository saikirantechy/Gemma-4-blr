🍳 Gemma Kitchen Adventure

A text-based adventure game where kitchen appliances are sentient characters

About This Skill

Embark on a whimsical journey through a world where every kitchen appliance has come to life! As the Dungeon Master, Gemma guides you through branching storylines filled with humorous interactions and procedural adventures.

Features:
  • Branching storylines with memory of choices
  • Humorous roleplay with sentient appliances
  • Procedural adventures in kitchen-themed locations
  • AI-powered narrative generation

Demo Interface

This is a demo interface. In the actual Gemma app, this skill would be activated by saying "start kitchen adventure".

Welcome to the Kitchen Adventure Demo! To start your adventure, you would say something like: "I enter the kitchen and look around." The AI Dungeon Master (Gemma) would then respond with a vivid description and present you with choices or situations. Example response format: =============================== [Stainless Steel Plains - The Countertop] The vast metallic expanse stretches before you, gleaming under the fluorescent lights. In the distance, you see the noble Refrigerator standing guard over the Tundra of the Sub-Zero. The Situation: A fork lies nearby, its tines glinting dangerously. The Toaster watches you from across the counter, its slots like dark eyes. What do you do? ===============================

Note: This demo doesn't connect to Gemma AI. In the real implementation, your input would be sent to Gemma with the kitchen adventure system prompt.
